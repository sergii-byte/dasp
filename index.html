Track 1

DASP Registration Flow

Two-phase process administered by CNAD. Estimated 8–12 months end-to-end. Each interaction cycle: CNAD has 20 business days to respond RPSAD Art. 8, applicant has 10 business days to cure RPSAD Art. 8. Expect 2–3 rounds of corrections.

Pre-Registration & First Presentation

Submit form to CNAD, present the product. CNAD issues "Objection" or "No Objection".

20 bd CNAD response
▾

Phase 2 — Definitive Registration

CNAD Review, Corrections & Final Meeting

Each round: 20 bd (CNAD) + 10 bd (applicant). Expect 2–3 rounds of feedback and supplementary submissions.

12–18 wk (2–3 rounds)
▾
  • CNAD review: 20 business days RPSAD Art. 8 LEAD Art. 20
  • Cure period if incomplete: 10 business days RPSAD Art. 8
  • Additional info requests before deadline, CNAD specifies term RPSAD Art. 8
  • CNAD considers market needs and applicant reputation RPSAD Art. 8
  • Each significant change: +5 business days RPSAD Art. 9
  • Final meeting: CNAD verifies CDD, Travel Rule, PEPs, UBO, training CNAD Guide §6.b

Timeline math: 1 round = 20 bd (CNAD review) + 10 bd (applicant cure) ≈ 6 calendar weeks. With 2–3 rounds: 12–18 weeks. Significant changes add +5 bd each RPSAD Art. 9. CNAD may extend any term by up to 50% RPSAD Art. 44.

Per round: 20 bd CNAD Per round: 10 bd applicant Per change: +5 bd Extension: up to +50%

Completion

Payment, Registration & Tax Benefits

Pay fee, receive registration number, activate full tax exemptions.

10 bd payment + 3 bd CNAD→MoF
▾
  • Registration fee: $6,132 (15 × $408.80 min. salary) within 10 business days from favorable resolution notification RPSAD Art. 10 LEAD Art. 12(1)(a)
  • If not paid in time → not considered registered RPSAD Art. 10
  • Definitive registration number assigned after payment verification RPSAD Art. 11
  • CNAD → Ministry of Finance within 3 business days after payment verification RPSAD Art. 11
  • Tax exemption qualification issued RPSAD Art. 11
  • Code of Ethics: 6 months from registration RPSAD Art. 18
0% Income Tax 0% VAT 0% Capital Gains 0% Municipal Tax

Note on cure periods: LEAD Art. 20 grants 5 business days to cure; RPSAD Art. 8 grants 10 working days. RPSAD is the more recent and specific regulation — in practice, 10 days applies. CNAD may also request additional info before the 20-day deadline expires, specifying a custom term RPSAD Art. 8. Any term can be extended by up to 50% RPSAD Art. 44.

Post-Registration Obligations: Annual renewal $4,088 (10 × $408.80), Q1 each year RPSAD Art. 12. Quarterly reports: first 20 days of Apr/Jul/Oct/Jan RPSAD Arts. 15–16. Non-payment = registration cancelled + tax benefits lost RPSAD Art. 12. Records retention: 15 years AML Law Art. 26.

Track 2

Issuer Registration Flow

Separate track for entities issuing digital assets. LEAD Arts. 22–32 and Issuer Regulation (CNAD-017-2023).

1

Issuer Registration

Register the entity with CNAD. May be simultaneous with issuance.

▾
Documents Issuer Reg. Art. 4
  • Sworn statement (notarized) Art. 4(a)
  • Articles of incorporation, amendments, bylaws Art. 4(b)
  • Shareholders: 10%+ with ID/TIN + UBO for corporates Art. 4(c)
  • Directors: ID, TIN, credentials, CV with experience Art. 4(d)
  • 3-year audited financials Art. 4(g)
  • TIN, website, jurisdictions, previous issuances Art. 4(h)–(k)
2

Prepare RID

Relevant Information Document per Annex A. Fair, clear, not misleading.

▾
  • < $1M → Spanish only; ≥ $1M → Spanish + English Art. 10
  • Summary/Cover: issuer info, platforms, amount, trading label Annex A
  • Body: 3-year IFRS financials, 21 offering sub-items (Debt/Equity/Income), fund destination, DLT technology, risk description & management, dispute resolution, tax regime
3

Certifier Report & CNAD Decision

Certifier evaluates RID → report to CNAD → decision within 5 business days.

▾
  • Certifier reviews RID, issues report with risk assessment Art. 14
  • Report to CNAD within 5 business days Art. 15(6)
  • CNAD decision: 5 business days Art. 16
  • If incomplete: 20 business days to cure Art. 16
  • Use qualification within 3 months Art. 17(a)
  • If no certifiers: CNAD certifies LEAD Art. 45
Issuance Documents Art. 5
  • Sworn statement + Board certification Art. 5(a)–(b)
  • Certifier's report, platforms, contracts, smart contract addresses + audit Art. 5(c)–(i)
  • Draft RID per Annex A Art. 5(j)
  • All relevant persons' identity info Art. 5(l)

Fee: 0.01% of offering amount + $50/certification LEAD Art. 12(2)

Security

Cybersecurity Requirements

RPSAD Arts. 25–26: governance, real-time monitoring, mandatory testing.

Governance Art. 25(a)

Internal procedures, external audits. CISO with authority and independence.

Identification Art. 25(b)

Safeguard mechanisms. Protect order management, custody, acquirer data systems.

Protection Art. 25(c)

SOC, IPS, access controls. Internal threat detection. Employee training.

Detection Art. 25(d)

Continuous real-time monitoring. Multi-level security controls.

Recovery Art. 25(d)(vi)

Critical processes: 6 hours. Transaction settlement: 24 hours.

Testing Art. 26

External auditor. 3 scenarios: confidentiality, availability, integrity.
