Regulatory Stack

The binding sources, in one view

The currently-binding statutes, regulations and supervisory rules cited across both guides.

Statute · DASP regime
LEAD

DASP statute (Decree 643/2023). Defines the PSAD legal status, regulated activities, CNAD authority and tax regime.

Licensing
Regulation · DASP
RPSAD

CNAD operational rulebook — registration, capital, governance, cybersecurity, ongoing reporting.

Licensing + Ops
Statute · AML/CTF · Decree 426/2025
LCLDA

El Salvador AML statute. Sets reporting entities, CDD triggers, the 24-h STR clock, 15-year retention and tipping-off prohibition.

Compliance
Operational rules · UIF
Instructivo UIF

UIF operational AML rulebook (Reform 1 + Reform 2). Covers CDD/EDD/SDD, PEP, BO, Travel Rule and VASP-specific obligations (Arts. 84-A/B/C).

Compliance
Statute · Counter-terrorism
LECAT

Decree 108 — counter-terrorism law. UNSC sanctions freeze (Art. 37) and terrorist designation regime.

Compliance
Operational guide · UIF
Acuerdo 734/2022 — UIF DASP Guide

UIF supervisory guide for digital-asset service providers. Sets the imperative DDC cascade (≤ $1K SDD · $1K–$4K Standard · > $4K EDD), monthly + individual transaction reports and the eight Anexo IV forms.
