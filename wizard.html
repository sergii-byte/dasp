Channel / Jurisdiction guide

Channel = the money-flow rail, not the asset direction. Crypto for any BTC/ETH/USDT etc. transfer. Bank wire for fiat electronic rails. Cash for physical money. Other for vouchers / gift-cards / money orders.

Jurisdiction: High-risk = FATF Grey List + partial sanctions regimes. Restricted = UN Security Council lists, OFAC comprehensive, national crypto-bans and FATF Call for Action. Lists verified Q1 2026 — refresh after each FATF plenary (Feb / Jun / Oct). Salvadoran statute binds UNSC + UIF national lists only; OFAC / EU / UK enter through banking-partner contracts and USD-corridor exposure.
